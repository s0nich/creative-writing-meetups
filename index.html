🐝 How a meet-up works

Four sweet steps to a finished piece.

Every session is hands-on and personalized to your child.

1

📖 Read together

We start with a short poem, flash fiction, or story.

2

💬 Talk about it

We zoom in on one cool choice the writer made.

3

✏️ Your kid writes

They write their own piece, then we discuss and edit.

4

✨ Polish it up

A finished piece your child will be proud of.
